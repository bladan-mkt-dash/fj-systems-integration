Golden Record — Identity Resolution Flow

When any team updates a patient or lead, webhooks hit the GCP Middleware Hub. The hub matches identity, updates the master record in BigQuery, and queues outbound sync. Empty fields are filled automatically across all three systems; existing AthenaNet values are never overwritten unless edited manually there.

Bi-directional inbound — fill-if-empty outbound to Athena Any team can be the source of a change: marketing in GHL, front desk in Zenoti, or clinical in AthenaNet. The hub resolves identity, writes the golden record once, then propagates data to every system.

AthenaNet rule: empty fields are filled automatically (e.g. credit card entered in Zenoti writes to a blank field in Athena). Once a field has data in AthenaNet, it is never overwritten by the hub — only a manual edit in Athena can change it.

Once implemented, staff enter shared data in only one system — no double or triple entry. A credit card, first name, or phone captured at the wellness center desk in Zenoti can flow into an empty Athena record automatically. GHL and Zenoti stay fully in sync for missing fields; Athena is protected once populated.
