Appointment Sync — Staff Calendar & Patient Record Flags

When an appointment is booked in AthenaNet or Zenoti, the hub runs two parallel outbound tracks: staff GHL calendars (staff only) and patient record flags in GHL and Zenoti — patients do not have calendars in those systems; their profiles are updated so appointment history is kept.

Example: clinical appt in Athena → GHL contact flagged + Zenoti guest profile flagged (when linked). Wellness appt in Zenoti → GHL contact flagged only. BigQuery golden record always stores the appointment for cross-system history.

Two tracks, one webhook — calendars are for staff only Patients do not have calendars in GHL or Zenoti. Track B updates their records so staff can see appointment history: flags, notes, activities, and a persistent log in BigQuery — not calendar slots.

Every linked patient profile must show that an appointment occurred and retain that history for marketing, front desk, and reporting — without re-booking in the destination system and without overwriting Athena clinical data once populated.

Track A (staff): GHL user calendar block — only when staff uses both Zenoti + GHL.
Track B (patient): record flag on GHL contact and/or Zenoti guest — skip the system where the appt was booked.

Calendar events should use busy blocks or de-identified labels unless GHL is confirmed HIPAA-aligned for full clinical detail on staff calendars.
